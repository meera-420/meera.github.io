Revive Candles are a collection of candles designed to rejuvenate and heal the soul. Using essential oils such as lavender, lemon grass, and so on.. By manipulating the sense of smell, we are able to help with healing and even realigning one's chakras. As human beings, we all deserve a reset. However, life is designed to constantly and relentlessly keep moving forward. Whether it's school, work, family, or even social activities. these things tend to drain a human's battery. That is why it it important to allow ourselves a moment to recharge and REVIVE.

ABOUT

REVIVE Candles was co-founded in 2022 by four undergraduate students. The concept was inspired in hopes of creating the best aromatic product that can be used by essentially Everyone. These four students had watched as their friends, peers, family, and even themselves struggle to maintain a sense of self after long days and weeks of work. That is why they came together to create this soul healing and REVIVING product.
